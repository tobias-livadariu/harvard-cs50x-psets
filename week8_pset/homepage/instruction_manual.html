Instruction Manual

Finally, I will teach you how to care for a dog.

Below is an ordered list containing some important tips for raising a dog.

Note that they are listed in order of importance.

I have included the concequences and potential risks that can occur if you, as a dog owner, deviate from this list.

(note that this list in no way teaches you how to actually care for a dog, and it is, as most people would put it, a complete "joke")

  1. 1. You must walk your dog for the first time at exactly 6:33am every day.
    • Consequence: You run the risk of your dog getting angry at you. This may then cause it to turn to capitalism, make millions of dollars, buy your house, and evict you.
  2. 2. You must feed your dog exactly 1.56 grams of protein per gram of body mass daily.
    • Consequence: If you feed your dog less protein, not much will happen. However, if you feed your dog more protein, it will become a super-bodybuilder, win the Mr. Olympia contest, and bench press your house.
  3. 3. You must play catch with your dog a minimum of 3 times per day, with a minimum duration of 13 minutes and 12 seconds per session of catch.
    • Consequence: Same as the first point on this list.
  4. 4. You must call your dog a "good boy/girl" at least 5 times per day.
    • Consequence: You run the risk of your dog devolving into a cat.
